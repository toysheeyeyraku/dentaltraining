Meet Dr Mark Hughes

Since graduating from internationally lauded Trinity College,  Dublin in 1992, Dr Mark Hughes has established a reputation  as a world-leader in cosmetic and restorative dentistry.

Mark’s achievements include more than 3000 hours of intensive postgraduate training. His alma maters include the Pankey Institute where his foundation for excellence was established. Mark passes on his expertise to fellow professionals and the next generation of dentists through his own Hughes Dental Training faculty.

  Mark has earned the prestigious British Association of Cosmetic Dentistry (BACD)-accreditation, a status held by only a handful of professionals worldwide. His commitment to providing the highest levels of care and satisfaction has been recognised by prominent dental brands including Align Technology, Sensodyne, and Kavo Kerr, for whom he has acted as a key opinion leader.

  Mark has founded several successful clinics, including on Harley Street, where he now consults at Harley Street Dental Studio. He is also co-founder of Define Clinic in Beaconsfield, where he works hand-in-hand with leading cosmetic surgery and aesthetics teams to deliver a uniquely collaborative approach to face and smile perfection.

  Perhaps the most significant achievement of all is a loyal following among patients, many of whom travel internationally for treatment with Mark. He is proud to have improved their quality of life, confidence, and happiness through his work.
