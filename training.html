Be Exceptional

Dr Mark Hughes’ dental training is a uniquely intimate learning pathway for  cosmetic and restorative dentists of ambition.

Over ten in-depth modules delivered to just ten delegates per course, Mark reveals some of the advanced methodologies he uses to tackle complex cases and maintain his position at the profession’s forefront.

Mark has invited world-leading voices in dentistry to share both clinical and entrepreneurial insight to support fellow professionals in reaching the apex of achievement.

Delegates hear from successful UK practice owners and BACD-accredited clinical leaders in the relaxed setting of Mark’s luxury Beaconsfield practice.

As well as examining hundreds of real-life case studies, the course features hands-on clinical learning with real patients.
